Avocado Salad

Home

Description:

This avocado salad is so good, and so easy to make! You can even switch some of the ingredients to your own liking.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 sweet onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 large ripe tomato
  • 1/4 fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients, and chop them up.
  2. Combine avocados, onion, bell pepper, tomato, cilantro, and lime juice in a large bowl.
  3. Gently toss until evenly coated. Season with salt and pepper.
