Homemade Pizza

This homemade pizza is perfect for beginners. The pizza dough only has 5 ingredients! Look below for all the ingredients needed and the instructions.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
